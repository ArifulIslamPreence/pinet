Our Team

Dr.Israat Haque

Assistant Professor, Faculty of Computer Science

Dr.Israat Haque is an assistant professor in the Faculty of Computer Science at Dalhousie University, Halifax, where, She leads Programmable and Intelligent Networking (PINet) research group. PINet members work in the areas of Software-Defined Networking (SDN) and the Internet of Things (IoT) focused on the monitoring, performance, security, and reliability aspects of wired and wireless networked systems. Dr. Israat Haque is also interested in applying data-driven approaches to solve practical and relevant problems.

Dr.Israat Haque received her Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computing Science at the University of Alberta, and subsequently held an NSERC post-doctoral position at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of California, Riverside, before joining Dalhousie University. She is actively involved in the organizing committee of IEEE NetSoft, IEEE ICNP, ACM CAN-CWiC, and IFIP Networking. Furthermore, Dr.Israat Haque an area editor of the IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology.
